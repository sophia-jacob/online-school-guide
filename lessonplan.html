Google Slides Theme Tutorial

This lesson will walk you through the steps to create Google Slides with specific themes.

1

Step 1

Go to google, and search up, "slide themes"

2

Step 2

Press enter, and you will see a bunch of links. Most likely, press the first one! You also have options to press the others, but you can choose! We will choose SlidesGo

3

Step 3

Click on the preferred link of your choice, then, you will see somewhat of a page like this!

4

Step 4

Choose a theme, that you like!

5

Step 5

Click on the theme, to see a page like this!

6

Step 6

On the right lower corner, you will see a button that says, "Use a Google Slides Theme", we will use that! Underneath that button, there is another button that says, "Download a PowerPoint Template", you can also choose that option. But we will go with the Google Slides option!

7

Step 7

Click on the button, and you will see this page!

8

Step 8

In the middle of the screen, you will see a button that says, "make a copy", click on that!

9

Step 9

There you go! You will get your own slides, along with a cool theme!

Step 10
